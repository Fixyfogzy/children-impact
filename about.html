Who We Are

A community-driven organization dedicated to creating lasting change through education, health initiatives, and empowerment programs for children and families in underserved communities.

Our Journey of Impact

Founded in 2010, Children Impact & Dev. Initiative (CIDI) began as a small community project in Lagos, Nigeria. What started as an after-school program for 30 children has grown into a comprehensive organization serving thousands across multiple regions.

Our founders, a group of educators and community leaders, recognized the transformative power of education and holistic support in breaking cycles of poverty.

Our Guiding Principles

The values that drive our work and define our approach to creating lasting change

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable children and families through quality education, healthcare access, and community development programs that break cycles of poverty and create sustainable futures.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to education, healthcare, and opportunities to reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Community, Empowerment, and Sustainability guide every decision we make and every program we implement.
