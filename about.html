Who We Are

A community-driven organization dedicated to creating lasting change through education, health initiatives, and empowerment programs for children and families in underserved communities.

Our Journey of Impact

Children Impact and Development Initiative (CIDI) is a non-govermental and non-profit organization dully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission since 2011 with the passion of moulding the future generation. We appreciate the fact that they are the necessary tools for a virile society and therefore provide support for their welfare and development mentally, socially, medically and otherwise. We prepare them to provide answers and solutions to the challenges of our society as they attain their full potentials We therefore, engage and encourage stakeholders (government, policy makers, communities, parents and guardians) to work together to ensure that children 's rights are protected and that they are given the necessary opportunities to thrive. .

The challenges have become greater, indeed more apparent and requires for all hands to be on deck if we must enjoy the future that we desire. We have been contributing to improving and impacting lives for over 2 decades. Our activities are funded through the sales of my Books and free will support from family and friends. It is my belief that you will be part of this vision .

Our Guiding Principles

The values that drive our work and define our approach to creating lasting change

Our Mission

To ensure the transformation of a society where Women and Children can thrive; from self to society.

Our Vision

Transforming our world, one child, one family, one society through advocacy and social justice.

Our Values

We are committed to making responsible choices that foster positive influence and building resilience in curbing social ills to improve society. Our efforts center on providing a voice for the marginalized and vulnerable, while engaging time, talent, and treasure in service of humanity.

Our Leaders

Executive Director
