About CIDI

Children Impact and Development Initiative (CIDI) is a non-governmental, non-profit organization that empowers children through welfare and development support, advocating for their rights and opportunities to thrive.

Founded in 2010, we've reached over 5,000 children, providing them with the tools and opportunities they need to break the cycle of poverty and build a better future.
